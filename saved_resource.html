About GerBan Fibres

GerBan Fibres Ltd stands as a pioneering force in the natural fiber industry, specializing in the processing and supply of premium jute fibers. Based in Bangladesh, we leverage our strategic location in one of the world's primary jute-growing regions.

Our commitment to 100% natural processing ensures that we maintain the inherent strength and sustainability of jute while meeting the demanding requirements of modern composite and nonwoven industries.

With decades of expertise, we have established ourselves as a trusted partner for businesses worldwide, delivering consistent quality, reliability, and innovation in natural fiber solutions.
